Undergraduate Programmes

Three routes into modern computer science learning.

The undergraduate page now compares the department's main pathways directly so applicants can understand which option matches their goals and background.

  • BCA application-focused
  • B.Sc. CS science-oriented
  • B.Sc. DS data-driven
BCA

Bachelor of Computer Applications

For students who want an applied route into programming, software development, databases, and web-oriented technical work.

Ideal for: learners who want to build software skills early and move confidently toward application development roles.

Typical focus: programming, software workflows, databases, and practical assignments.

  • Programming and problem-solving basics
  • Database and web development exposure
  • Hands-on, application-friendly direction
B.Sc. CS

B.Sc. Computer Science

For science-background students looking for deeper conceptual grounding in algorithms, systems, computing logic, and disciplined laboratory study.

Ideal for: students who prefer a structured science pathway with strong foundations in computing theory and practice.

Typical focus: data structures, systems, networking, and analytical thinking.

  • Core computing fundamentals
  • Algorithmic and systems understanding
  • Preparation for advanced academic progression
B.Sc. DS

B.Sc. Data Science

For learners interested in data handling, analysis, interpretation, and the foundations that support AI and machine learning study.

Ideal for: students who enjoy numbers, analysis, patterns, and technology-led decision support.

Typical focus: data workflows, analytics, statistics-aware thinking, and emerging tool use.

  • Data analysis orientation
  • Foundations for machine learning study
  • Project-friendly modern discipline

ADMISSION PROCESS

How to Apply for Admission

  1. Explore Available Programmes

    Review the undergraduate and postgraduate programmes offered by the department and select the course that best aligns with your academic interests and career goals.

  2. Verify Eligibility Criteria

    Check the eligibility requirements, reservation policies, and admission guidelines prescribed by t he university and government authorities before applying.

  3. Submit Required Documents

    Keep all necessary documents ready, including mark sheets, transfer certificate, caste certificate (if applicable), Aadhaar card, passport-size photographs, and other supporting documents.

  4. Complete Admission Formalities

    Visit the college admission office, complete the verification process, submit the required forms, and confirm your admission as per the academic schedule.
