Postgraduate Programme

M.Sc. Computer Science: Advanced Learning, Research, and Innovation.

The M.Sc. Computer Science programme is designed to provide advanced knowledge in computing technologies, research methodologies, software development, data analytics, artificial intelligence, and emerging technological domains. The programme prepares students for higher education, research careers, and leadership roles in the IT industry.

  • Advanced Curriculum Comprehensive study of modern computing technologies and advanced computer science concepts.
  • Project Work Industry-oriented projects, seminars, dissertations, and practical research experience.
  • Research Focus Encouraging innovation, analytical thinking, and scientific research in emerging areas of technology.
PROGRAMME OVERVIEW

A Pathway to Advanced Technical Expertise

The M.Sc. Computer Science programme provides in-depth knowledge of software engineering, data science, artificial intelligence, machine learning, cloud computing, cybersecurity, and research methodologies. Students gain strong analytical, technical, and problem-solving skills required for professional and academic excellence.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Computer Applications, or an equivalent discipline from a recognized university.
  • Candidates must satisfy the eligibility criteria prescribed by the affiliating university and regulatory authorities.
  • Admissions are granted according to university norms, reservation policies, and institutional guidelines.
Curriculum Flow

Semester-wise structure

Semester I

Core strengthening

  • Computer Architecture & Microprocessor
  • Python Programming
  • Advanced Java
  • Research Methodology
  • Power BI / Statistical Method / Web Technology
Semester II

Emerging Technologies

  • Mobile App Development with Kotlin
  • Cloud Computing
  • NoSQL with MongoDB
  • Industry Internship / Field Project
  • Data Structure / Software Testing / PHP & MySQL
Semester III

Advanced Computing

  • Image Processing using Python
  • Hibernate & Spring Framework
  • Blockchain Technology
  • Research Project
  • Data Science / Linux Admin / Networks / IoT
Semester IV

Specialization & Research

  • Web Application with MVC Core
  • Introduction to AI & ML
  • Publication Ethics
  • Major Research Project
  • DBA / Data Warehousing / DevOps
Postgraduate Value

What learners can expect from the PG route

Advanced computing depth

Students move beyond introductory skill-building into stronger technical analysis and system understanding.

Research orientation

Methodology, seminars, and dissertation-style work help learners organize independent academic thinking.

Project responsibility

Major work in later semesters supports design, implementation, documentation, and presentation skills.

Career and higher-study readiness

The overall pathway fits students looking at advanced industry roles, academic growth, or future research.
